We make visits to people's homes while they are out of town on business or on vacation for the purpose of caring for their pets and homes. During these visits we will feed, play, exercise, administer medication if necessary and give lots of love to your pet(s). We can also bring in your mail and newspaper, turn lights on, take out the trash and water your indoor plants. These visits will be totally tailored to meet the needs of you and your pet(s). If you prefer not to leave your pet(s) alone, a pet sitter can stay overnight in your home. Alternatively, you can board your dog or cat at the home of one of our professional pet sitters. We also provide daily dog walking. This service works great for people who work long hours or don't have the time to walk their dogs. If you decide to use a professional dog walker and/or pet sitter, we will arrange a time to meet with you and your pet(s). During this initial get together, we can get important information about how to best care for your pet(s) and home. We will have you fill out some paperwork, have you show us around your home and obtain a copy of the key to your home. We've taken extra steps to ensure that you can feel comfortable leaving your pet(s) and home in the hands of a qualified professional.
